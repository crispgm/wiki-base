# Pan Slavic Names

## Family Names

- _-ов_ (-ov), _-ев_ (-yev): meaning "belonging to" or "of the clan of/descendant of".
- _-ский_ (-sky): an adjectival form meaning "associated with" and usually used for toponymic surnames.
- _-ović/-ević_, _-ić_: patronyms
