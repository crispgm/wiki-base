为什么南非白人的姓氏很集中？

index > history > south-africa

Many people of European heritage in South Africa are descended from Huguenots. Most of these originally settled in the Cape Colony, but were absorbed into the Afrikaner and Afrikaans-speaking population, because they had religious similarities to the Dutch colonists. See Huguenots in South Africa.
