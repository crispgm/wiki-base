Family Names

  • -ов (-ov), -ев (-yev): meaning “belonging to” or “of the clan of/descendant of”.
  • -ский (-sky): an adjectival form meaning “associated with” and usually used for toponymic surnames.
  • -ović/-ević, -ić: patronyms
